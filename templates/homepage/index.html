Empower Your Health — Be Your Own Doctor

There are moments when immediate medical attention is essential, but reaching a doctor might not always be possible. HEALURIX bridges this gap by offering an AI-driven disease prediction and consultation platform that provides instant, reliable guidance based on your symptoms.

Our system is built to support end-users with accurate disease predictions and online consultations. Users can input their health symptoms, and the application uses intelligent machine learning algorithms to analyze the input and determine the most likely illness. It has been trained on a comprehensive dataset mapping symptoms to various diseases to ensure accurate predictions.

Once a potential diagnosis is provided, users receive a detailed report including the disease description, recommended medications, precautions, suitable workouts, and dietary suggestions. Additionally, HEALURIX offers a doctor consultation feature where users can connect with relevant specialists and download prescriptions submitted by doctors after consultation.

With HEALURIX, proactive healthcare is at your fingertips — helping you make informed decisions and take timely action toward better health.
